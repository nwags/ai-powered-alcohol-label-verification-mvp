# Policy Alignment Notes (Cautious, Non-Legal)

This note is informational only. It is **not** a formal legal, procurement, privacy, security, accessibility, records-management, or compliance determination.

## Directional Alignment Observation

At a high level, this MVP appears directionally aligned with practical federal AI adoption themes because it emphasizes:

- human-in-the-loop decision support,
- local execution and operational control,
- transparent reviewer-facing outputs,
- incremental deployment through a bounded prototype.
